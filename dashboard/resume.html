Resume - Mark Hoel

My name is Mark Hoel and I am a recently retired computer science, design and geography teacher from Toronto. I enjoy working in roles where I am creative, interactive and work with data about real people and places.

I started my career in Redlands, California working as a product specialist for ESRI, the world's largest GIS software vendor. Eventually I got married and moved back to Ontario and worked as a programmer for Akanda Innovation, a ground-breaking start-up eventually aquired by the Fortune 500 company, Tyler Technologies. Finally, I moved into education as a teacher at Upper Canada College, one of Canada's oldest and most presitious all boys private schools.

During my professional career, I have taken time out to complete a masters and start a PhD, as well as take a year off to travel the world with my family.

For more information, please take a look at my two page resume (pdf)

Summary

I have been lucky to have had a number of high quality experiences in my life and work with organizations that are recognized leaders in their fields. At the same time, I have been able to indulge my passions for geography and urban planning, research, data and data visualization as well as GIS and programming.
